In this newsletter, I will not only post a list of links as usual but also tie some of them together in a bit of a small opinion piece.

The deterioration of international order is, in my view, highly problematic as the advances in AI will most likely lead to huge social disruptions,
which require more international consensus and not more conflict. This becomes most apparent when discussing AI in military use cases and existential risk 
from the uncontrolled development of potentially super-intelligent unaligned agents. These issues are no longer in the far future but are already affecting policy right now.
