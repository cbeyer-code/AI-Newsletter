# AI-Newsletter
I will try to report current news and AI topics here in an unfiltered way.
This means the sources will have varying degrees of reliability and quality. 
It will definitely happen that some of the news will turn out to be false, overhyped, or otherwise
misleading, and I will not have the time to redact and clarify that in all cases, but I will try to do my best if I become aware of it. 

# Disclaimer:
In this newsletter, I try to gather recent news and events around AI and how it might impact different aspects of society and the environment. I will also occasionally share my current personal opinion about some of the presented information, which can, of course, be wrong and therefore subject to change in the future. These views are my own, and I want to make clear that I do not speak for any of my colleagues or other parts of the university.
