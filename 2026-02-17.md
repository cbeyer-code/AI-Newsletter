[https://www.youtube.com/shorts/3fYiLXVfPa4](https://www.youtube.com/shorts/3fYiLXVfPa4)

The 'Are you sure?' problem happens when LLMs flip their opinion based on that simple question because it implies the user might not have been satisfied with the answers, and making the user happy is more important to the model than telling the 'truth' as a result of the training. (blog)

[https://www.randalolson.com/2026/02/07/the-are-you-sure-problem-why-your-ai-keeps-changing-its-mind/](https://www.randalolson.com/2026/02/07/the-are-you-sure-problem-why-your-ai-keeps-changing-its-mind/)
