A panel of experts discussed the transition toward a hybrid AI-human workforce, where work shifts from execution to oversight. While AI agents might supercharge entrepreneurship, they risk erasing entry-level white-collar roles in admin, customer service, and legal work. A primary concern is that not everybody wants to be in a managerial role or an entrepreneur, and if AI handles all the grunt work, junior workers will lose the opportunity to learn the fundamentals, creating a gap that universities may need to fill. Without deep domain expertise, it becomes nearly impossible to judge whether AI output is high-quality or a hallucination, so keep evolving your competencies :-)
